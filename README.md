## About Me

I am Martin Demel, an IT professional with over 15 years of experience in service management, customer success, and operational transformation. I hold the ITIL 4 Managing Professional certification and currently work with cloud solutions, using AI to optimize code and operations in Kubernetes environments. My interest in AI in healthcare is deeply personal: after spending time in the NICU with my premature twin daughters, I became motivated to explore how technology can improve outcomes and save the lives of babies born prematurely. Throughout this course, I have focused my work on AI applications in neonatal intensive care.

## Course Overview

This course explores how artificial intelligence is applied across healthcare, covering foundational topics such as medical terminology, healthcare datasets, clinical decision support systems, diagnostic and prognostic modeling, non-traditional health data, risk identification, natural language processing, and reinforcement learning. Each assignment and discussion connects these concepts to real clinical settings, with an emphasis on responsible AI deployment.
