## Key Takeaway

AI in healthcare depends on more than algorithmic sophistication. Data quality, validation, interoperability, and transparency all condition whether a tool that performs well in research will function responsibly at the bedside. The NICU illustrates this clearly because its continuous monitoring, high-acuity decisions, and vulnerable patients amplify both the promise and the risk of every AI application.
